Part 1

Website — Pages, Structure & Content

Čisté šachty cleans ventilation and air ducts (vzduchové a odvětrávací šachty) in panel and apartment buildings — not sewage or drainage. The primary buyers are SVJ boards, bytová družstva, and property management firms, not individual homeowners. The site is deliberately a focused, 9-page build rather than a large SEO content site — one clear service, explained well, with dedicated pages for each buyer type. Two conversion features run across the site: an interactive price calculator on the homepage and a best-price guarantee ("Garance nejlepší ceny") featured on the homepage and the pricing page.

Domain: cistesachty.cz — short, memorable, matches the brand name exactly, and contains the primary keyword.
